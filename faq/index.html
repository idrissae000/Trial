What is your refund policy?

We only grant refunds if requested before our work has started. Once work has started, we do not offer refunds.

If you have questions before we begin, contact us and we’ll help.

How is my campaign fee distributed between Idriss Music Promos and editors?

35% of the total campaign fee is retained by Idriss Music Promos for management, coordination, and operations. The remaining 65% is distributed to editors as payment for delivering the edits.

How much do editors get paid per edit?

Editors are paid a flat $10 per edit, plus a performance bonus of $1 for every 1,000 views above 10,000 views. Bonuses are capped so the maximum payout per edit is $20 total.

Performance bonuses depend on views.
